Security Awareness

10 Controls Beginner Level Human Factor

Security training, phishing simulation, social engineering defense, and human risk management for building security-conscious organizational culture.

Overview

Security awareness forms the human firewall of cybersecurity defense. It transforms employees from potential security risks into active defenders through education, training, and cultural change. A well-designed security awareness program addresses the fact that up to 95% of successful cyber attacks are due to human error.

Modern security awareness goes beyond annual training sessions to create continuous learning experiences that change behavior. Effective programs use psychology, gamification, and real-world simulations to make security training engaging and memorable.
