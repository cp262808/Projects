<summary>IAM → Federation &amp; Conditional Access</summary>
<p class="tags"><b>Tags:</b> SSO, MFA</p>
<p><b>Question:</b> Is workforce identity federated to cloud/SaaS with conditional access and phishing-resistant MFA?</p>
<p><b>Applicable Requirements:</b><br/>- NIST 800-53: IA-2, AC-17<br/>- ISO 27001: A.9.2.1<br/>- SOC 2: CC6.1</p>
<p><b>Applicability:</b> Cloud consoles and SaaS</p>
<p><b>Expected Result:</b> No local users; SSO only; FIDO2/WebAuthn for admins; risk/device policy gates.</p>
<p><b>Why It Matters:</b> Eliminates password reuse and reduces phish success.</p>
<p><b>Technical Breakdown:</b></p>
<ol>
<li>Disable direct logins; enforce CA; monitor impossible travel.</li>
</ol>

<p><b>Applicable Requirements:</b><br/>- NIST 800-53: IA-5, AC-6<br/>- SOC 2: CC6.1</p>
<p><b>Applicability:</b> Automation and integrations</p>
<p><b>Expected Result:</b> No shared creds; unique identities; short-lived tokens; PAM/secret manager rotation.</p>
<p><b>Why It Matters:</b> Shared/long-lived secrets are high-risk.</p>
<p><b>Technical Breakdown:</b></p>
<ol>
<li>SCIM/IGA lifecycle; vault credentials; per-service roles.</li>
